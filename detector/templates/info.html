Our Story

In the science-a-thon hackathon, a team of 6 students came together to tackle the issue of fake news. They brainstormed and decided to create a web application that could detect and flag fake news articles using AI models. The team worked tirelessly, researching and experimenting with various algorithms and techniques to build a robust and accurate system. Finally, after hours of coding, testing, and refining, they had a functional prototype ready. The web application was user-friendly, intuitive, and had an impressive accuracy rate in detecting fake news articles. The team presented their creation to the judges and received enthusiastic feedback. They were thrilled to have developed a tool that could make a difference in the fight against misinformation.
